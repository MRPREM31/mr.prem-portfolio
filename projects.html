Gamified Digital Platform for Sustainable Farming

Published: September 20, 2025 Last updated: October 5, 2025

A gamified digital platform that transforms sustainable farming into an interactive and rewarding experience. This project motivates farmers through missions, points, badges, and leaderboards while providing localized, offline-first access.

Key Features
  • Farmer Profile: Personalized challenges based on location and crop type
  • Dashboard: Sustainability score, progress bar, and badges
  • Missions & Challenges: Interactive tasks for sustainable practices
  • Leaderboard: Community ranking to promote healthy competition
  • Rewards & Badges: Digital recognition and real-world benefits
  • Community Sharing: Farmers can share progress and inspire peers
  • Offline-first: Works in low-connectivity rural areas
  • Multilingual: Support for Odia, Hindi, and English
Technologies Used
  • Frontend: React, TypeScript, Vite
  • Backend: Node.js, JavaScript
  • Database: SQLite
  • Offline-first architecture with data sync
  • Mobile-friendly UI for rural farmers
